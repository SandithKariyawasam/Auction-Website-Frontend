Introduction

Welcome to our vehicle auction platform, where buyers and sellers connect through live auctions. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to safeguarding the personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, store, and use your data when you use our services, including features like real-time auctions, winner announcements, bidder history, and multiple currency support. By using our platform, you agree to the terms outlined below.

Definition of personal data

Personal data refers to any information that can identify you, such as:

  • Account information: Username, password, and profile preferences.
  • Auction activity: Bid history, items on your watchlist, and auction wins.
  • Financial data: Currency preference and payment-related information.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and device information.
  • We collect only the data necessary to provide services such as winner announcements, watchlist management, and bidder history tracking.
Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our platform uses cookies to enhance your experience and ensure smooth functionality during real-time auctions. Cookies allow us to:

You can remove or reject cookies using your browser or device settings, but in some cases doing so may affect your ability to use our products and services.
  • Maintain your watchlist and show personalized auction updates.
  • Keep track of your bid history across auctions.
  • Recognize your currency preferences for seamless transactions.
  • Collect usage statistics to improve our marketing and services.
  • You can manage or disable cookies via your browser settings, but doing so may limit some platform features.
Your Rights

Access: You can request to view the personal data we hold about you. Correction: If your data is inaccurate, you can request corrections. Deletion: You have the right to ask for your data to be deleted, subject to applicable legal or regulatory obligations. Objection: You can object to the processing of your data for marketing purposes or analytical use. Data Portability: We can provide your bid history or account data in a portable format upon request.
