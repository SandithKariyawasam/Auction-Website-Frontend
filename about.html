About us

Welcome to our Vehicle Auction website, a project developed by second-year, second-semester students from NSBM Green University as part of our group coursework. This platform was created by a team of six dedicated students with a shared vision of providing an efficient and user-friendly vehicle auctioning experience for Sri Lankan people.


Although each of us had specific roles, all members contributed to both the frontend and backend development of the project. We are proud undergraduates from NSBM Green University, and this website reflects our skills, teamwork, and passion for technology.


This auction website is designed to serve the people of Sri Lanka, providing a seamless platform to buy and sell vehicles online.
